# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment / 我们的承诺

中文：为了让 CodexRemote 社区对所有人都更友好、更安全，我们承诺营造一个尊重、包容、建设性的协作环境。  
English: In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming community, we are committed to making participation in CodexRemote respectful, inclusive, and constructive for everyone.

## Expected Behavior / 期望行为

中文：
- 尊重不同背景和经验水平的参与者
- 以建设性方式提出反馈
- 讨论问题时聚焦代码、文档和事实
- 接受不同观点，保持耐心

English:
- Show respect for people with different backgrounds and experience levels
- Give feedback constructively
- Keep discussions focused on code, docs, and facts
- Be patient and open to different perspectives

## Unacceptable Behavior / 不可接受的行为

中文：
- 人身攻击、羞辱、骚扰或歧视性言论
- 恶意挑衅、反复争吵或故意破坏讨论
- 泄露他人的私人信息
- 任何让协作环境变得不安全的行为

English:
- Personal attacks, harassment, or discriminatory language
- Deliberate trolling, repeated hostility, or disruptive behavior
- Sharing someone else’s private information without permission
- Any behavior that makes collaboration feel unsafe

## Scope / 适用范围

中文：本行为准则适用于项目 issue、pull request、讨论区以及其他与项目相关的公开协作空间。  
English: This code of conduct applies to project issues, pull requests, discussions, and other public collaboration spaces related to the project.

## Enforcement / 执行方式

中文：如果你发现不当行为，请通过仓库维护者可见的方式反馈。维护者会根据具体情况采取适当处理措施。  
English: If you observe unacceptable behavior, please report it through a channel visible to the repository maintainer. The maintainer will review and respond as appropriate.

## Interpretation / 解释

中文：维护者保留对本行为准则的解释权，并可根据项目发展进行更新。  
English: The maintainer reserves the right to interpret and update this code of conduct as the project evolves.
